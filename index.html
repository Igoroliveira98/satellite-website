Technology And Visibility

Our satellite uses the latest technology, always seeking to combine advances in this field with sustainability, bringing a better experience and safety to our products. One of the major improvements in its structure is present in the reduction of raw material, using recyclable material thus making the satellite lighter and less problematic in its maintenance.

Vision of Earth

The technology used in our cameras allows an approximation of the earth twice as large as any other satellite ever launched, also applying the field of view in relation to space. This will be important not only for monitoring possible asteroids that are going against the earth, but it will also allow for domestic use by nations. Another improvement is present in its network, being able to connect and monitor a terrestrial territory five times larger than any other satellite ever launched.
